My name is Ray Cardillo and I established Cardillo’s Creations as an indie software company in 2008.  I am a one man shop, but I’m unofficially supported by family and friends.  I am already an accomplished software engineer, and I am always dedicated to my full-time job, so this is more of a hobby for me than anything else.  Calling it a hobby is a little odd given the number of hours that I spend creating and supporting my projects, but it really is a form of recreation for me, so I’m alright with that.  Some people work on classic cars or build models… I do that kinda stuff as well… but mostly I do this.
